About Arab Contractors

Seven Decades of Engineering Excellence

OUR STORY

History & Heritage

The Arab Contractors (Osman Ahmed Osman & Co.) is one of the leading construction companies in the Middle East and Africa. Our roots extend back more than half a century, founded in 1955 by the visionary engineer Osman Ahmed Osman.

From the construction of the Aswan High Dam to the world's widest cable-stayed bridge, our history is a testament to our ability to tackle the most complex engineering challenges.

Mission

To provide high-quality construction services by applying the latest international standards in quality and safety to achieve sustainable development goals.

Vision

To be a global leader in the construction industry, contributing to infrastructure development with an innovative vision that meets future needs.

Values

Integrity, Quality, Safety, and Sustainability are the pillars of our operations and the foundation of our success.
